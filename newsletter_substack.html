UCLA Runs South Carolina Off The Court For First Championship

UCLA didn't just beat South Carolina. They demolished them, 79-51, in the third-largest championship margin in history.

https://twitter.com/SportsCenter/status/2040910147397075027

This was UCLA's first NCAA title. Their first championship game appearance since the tournament began in 1982. Forty-eight years since their AIAW title.

https://twitter.com/ClutchPoints/status/2040915510880321928

The numbers are absurd. The 25-9 third quarter was the largest single-quarter margin in championship history. UCLA outrebounded them 49-37. South Carolina shot 13% from three.

https://twitter.com/ClutchPoints/status/2040917102664745335

The Big Ten hasn't won a women's basketball title since 1999. UCLA, in just their second season in the conference, broke a 27-year drought. And Michigan gets their shot at ending the men's drought Monday.
