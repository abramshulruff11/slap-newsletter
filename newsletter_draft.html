Dexter Lawrence Demands Trade from Giants After Contract Standoff

All-Pro defensive tackle Dexter Lawrence wants out of New York.
@AdamSchefter
ESPN sources: Giants All-Pro defensive tackle Dexter Lawrence has requested a trade and he will not be participating in the team's off-season workout program that opens Tuesday. Lawrence and the Giants have been through two off seasons attempting to negotiate a contract reflecting his value to the Giants over the last three years, but there has not been any progress, per sources.

Giannis and Bucks in Full-Scale War: 'It Feels Like a Funeral'

The Giannis-Bucks situation has gone completely nuclear. An NBA insider described the team atmosphere with a quote that belongs in a museum: "It feels like a funeral." This isn't load management drama anymore. This is a franchise star in open warfare with his organization. Giannis publicly declared himself healthy against the team's medical staff. Bobby Portis reportedly erupted at practice with "This is why we suck." The Bucks went 4-1 without Giannis late in the season before getting eliminated from the playoffs on March 28. A $275 million extension is eligible in October. Trade rumors have involved the Timberwolves, Heat, and Warriors. "It feels like a funeral" is going to be the quote that defines this entire season.
