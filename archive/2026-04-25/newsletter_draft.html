LeBron at 41 Throws a Lob to His Son Then Hits the Shot to Force OT

LeBron James is 41 years old. He played 45 minutes in a playoff overtime game. He threw an alley-oop to his son. Then he hit a game-tying three with 13.6 seconds left after the Rockets choked away a six-point lead.
